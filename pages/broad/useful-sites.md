


# Useful Websites For Developers

## [Temp Mail](https://temp-mail.org/)
Free online temporary email inbox, it allows you to create an email address and receive emails in it, it is extremely useful for accessing content requiring you to sign in using you email.


## [CORS Proxy](https://corsproxy.io/)
Free proxy that allows you to access data from different websites in your own website without being blocked by the CORS (Cross-Origin Resource Sharing) policy in the browser.
